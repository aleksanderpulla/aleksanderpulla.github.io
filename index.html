About

Aleksandër Pulla recently got certified by Amazon for AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner (CCP), as well as continues the cloud journey to learn the competences and skills of AWS Solutions Architect.

He is currently working as a full-time Solutions Engineer at CData Software (after transitioning from Technical Engineer in the same company as a result of a promotion).
Before that, he has been working as a full-time lecturer at the European University of Tirana, Faculty of Engineering, Informatics & Technology

In 2020 he graduated in Master of Science in Computer Science with specialization in Software Engineering, Mälardalen University, Sweden. One year ago he also graduated in Master Of Science in Business Informatics, European University of Tirana, Albania.
In 2015-2018 he finished his Bachelor in Finance & Accounting, University of Tirana, Albania.
